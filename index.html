Welcome to Bethany

✛

A place of grace, belonging, and worship — open to all who seek God.

We are a congregation of the Presbyterian Church (U.S.A.), rooted in the Reformed tradition and gathered in the heart of Ontario, Oregon. Whether you are a lifelong believer or simply curious, you are welcome here. We invite you to join us for worship, fellowship, and service in our community.
