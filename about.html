Who We Are

✛

Bethany Presbyterian Church has been a presence in the Ontario, Oregon community for generations. We are a congregation of the Presbyterian Church (U.S.A.), united by faith in Jesus Christ and a shared calling to love our neighbors and serve our community.

We are a diverse family of believers — young and old, long-time members and first-time visitors. Whatever your story, there is room for you here. We believe that the church is at its best when all voices are welcomed and all people are valued.

Our congregation gathers each Sunday for worship, and throughout the week for fellowship, study, and service. We are connected to a broader network of Presbyterian congregations across the Treasure Valley and beyond.

What We Believe

✛

Our faith is rooted in Scripture, expressed in the Reformed tradition, and lived out in community.

Scripture

We hold the Bible as the authoritative Word of God, the rule of faith and practice for our lives together.

Grace

We believe that salvation is a gift of God's grace through faith in Jesus Christ — not earned by our own merit, but freely given to all who receive it.

Worship

Weekly worship — shaped by Word and Sacrament — is at the center of our life as a congregation. We gather to hear God speak and to respond in praise.

Community

We are called not to worship in isolation, but together — as a body of believers accountable to one another and to God.

Service

Faith without works is incomplete. We seek to serve our neighbors in Ontario and the surrounding communities as an expression of God's love.

Welcome

All are welcome at Bethany. No matter your background or where you are on your journey, you will find an open door here.

Bethany Presbyterian Church is a congregation of the Presbyterian Church (U.S.A.), one of the largest Presbyterian denominations in the United States. Our congregation is guided by the Book of Order and the Book of Confessions.
