Privacy Policy

What this website collects, and what it does not.

Last updated: April 2026

Bethany Presbyterian Church takes the privacy of our visitors seriously. This page explains plainly what information is collected when you visit bethanypresbyterianontario.com, who collects it, and why. We do not sell, share, or trade visitor data with any third party for advertising purposes.

This website does not use cookies

✛

No tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or persistent identifiers are placed on your device by this website. You will never see a cookie consent banner here because none are required.

Anonymous visit counting (GoatCounter)

✛

This website uses GoatCounter, a privacy-respecting, open-source analytics tool, to count page visits. GoatCounter does not use cookies and does not collect personal information. It records only:

  • Which page was visited
  • Approximate country (derived from your IP address, which is then discarded)
  • Browser type and operating system
  • The website or search engine that referred you here

Your IP address is used only to estimate your country and is never stored. No individual visitor can be identified from the data GoatCounter collects. You can read GoatCounter's own privacy policy at goatcounter.com/help/privacy.

Fonts (Google Fonts)

✛

The typefaces used on this site are loaded from Google Fonts. When your browser downloads these fonts, Google's servers receive your IP address and browser information as part of the normal request. Google states that it does not use this data for advertising profiling. You can read Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

Maps (Google Maps)

✛

The homepage and contact page include an embedded Google Maps frame showing the church's location. When that section of the page loads, Google's servers receive your IP address and may set cookies according to their own privacy policy. If you prefer not to interact with the embedded map, the church's address is listed separately on the contact page and can be entered into any mapping application of your choice.

Video (YouTube)

✛

Some pages on this site include embedded YouTube videos. We use YouTube's privacy-enhanced mode (youtube-nocookie.com), which means YouTube does not set tracking cookies on your device unless you choose to play a video. If you do play a video, YouTube's standard privacy policy applies. You can read it at policies.google.com/privacy.

Contact by email

✛

If you choose to contact us by email, your message and email address will be received by the church office and used only to respond to your enquiry. We do not add you to any mailing list or share your contact information without your explicit permission.

Questions

✛

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact the church office at bethanypresoffice@gmail.com or by phone at +1 (208) 739-6320.
